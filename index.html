Srinivas Vardhineni

I don't bother to give review unless I feel its too worth to share the info. I am giving the review since I feel its worth sharing this info and hope it helps someone.

Our son is of developmental delay, our genetic counselor suggested us to go for IVF after some genetic tests, and she suggested "Prasads Infertility Solutions"

Initially we were a bit speculative about proceeding with IVF here, since we not aware or heard about Prasads Infertility Solutions. Since genetic counselor suggested, we decided to procced with IVF here

After initial discussions with Dr. Suma Prasad, we felt a bit comfortable and convinced, and got some confidence, but somewhere in the mind, thoughts wrangling about "did we come to the right place?"

The procedure started and from the stage of egg retrieval, we started discussing with Suma prasad regarding the progress, and in the next subsequent few discussions, Suma prasad has become like our Family friend, its because, she never comes straight to the topic, she opens an informal conversation, and slowly she dwells into topic, and explains the progress of procedure, and she clarifies whatever we ask. She answered all the queries with lot of patience, though few queries from our side didn't make any sense at all. She has the wider outlook on many other general things apart from her profession

I remember she was explaining bit and piece of test results and progress, though its not a compulsion for her to do it

Initially, I was going along with my wife, but from the stage of embryo transfer, my wife was going alone by OLA/UBER cab as she felt very much comfortable with doctor and staff.

Things went well, and the delivery was also done by Suma prasad. We are blessed with a baby girl, now she is 3 years old, Whenever I spend time with my daughter, it recollects me of the experience with Prasads Infertility Solutions

Not last but not the least, staff is also very friendly, they were answering/clarifying all the questions over phone and when visited. I remember Tulasi and other lady at reception, were patiently listening to us and answering us. Thanks to staff

Thanks to Suma prasad for making our IVF journey comfortable
